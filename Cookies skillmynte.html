1. Introduction to Cookies

At SkillMynte, we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your learning experience on our AI-powered platform. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

Our Approach: We use cookies responsibly to improve your learning journey while respecting your privacy. By using SkillMynte, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

3. How We Use Cookies on SkillMynte

Cookies help us provide a personalized and efficient learning experience:

Enhanced User Experience

  • Keep you logged in during your learning sessions
  • Remember your course progress and resume where you left off
  • Save your preferences for language, theme, and display settings
  • Optimize page loading speeds based on your device and connection

Personalized Learning

  • Track your progress through coding courses (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc.)
  • Remember your interaction patterns with our AI-guided learning support
  • Suggest relevant courses based on your interests and skill level
  • Customize the difficulty of coding challenges based on your performance

Platform Improvement

  • Analyze which features are most used and which need improvement
  • Identify technical issues and optimize platform performance
  • Understand how users navigate through our courses and content
  • Measure the effectiveness of our AI learning support system

Learning Enhancement: Our analytics cookies help us understand how students learn most effectively, allowing us to continuously improve our course content and teaching methods.

4. Your Cookie Choices

You have control over how cookies are used on your device:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies (though this may break essential platform functions)
  • Delete existing cookies from your device
  • Set preferences for specific websites
  • Receive notifications when cookies are being set

How to Manage Cookies in Popular Browsers

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data

Mozilla Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Site permissions → Cookies and site data

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, please note that:

  • You may not be able to log in to your SkillMynte account
  • Your course progress may not be saved properly
  • Payment processing for subscriptions may not work correctly
  • The platform may not remember your preferences and settings
  • Some AI-guided learning features may not function optimally

Essential Cookies Required: Some cookies are necessary for SkillMynte to function properly. If you disable these, you may not be able to access certain features or complete your subscription payments.
