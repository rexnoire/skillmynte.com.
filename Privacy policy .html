1. Introduction

At SkillMynte, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our AI-powered learning platform.

By using SkillMynte, you consent to the practices described in this policy. We encourage you to read this document carefully to understand our approach to data privacy.

Our Commitment: We are dedicated to maintaining the trust and confidence of our users. We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data with other companies for marketing purposes.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best learning experience, we collect various types of information:

Personal Information

Your name, email address, and account details when you register.

Payment Information

Billing details and transaction history for subscription payments.

Learning Data

Your course progress, quiz results, and interaction with our AI tutor.

Technical Information

IP address, browser type, device information, and usage statistics.

Subscription Information

When you subscribe to SkillMynte, we collect information related to your chosen plan:

  • Monthly Plan (₦2,500): Payment details and subscription period
  • Yearly Plan (₦25,000): Payment details and annual renewal information
  • Lifetime Plan (₦800,000): One-time payment confirmation

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services:

Account Management

  • Create and manage your SkillMynte account
  • Authenticate your identity when you log in
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

Service Delivery

  • Process your subscription payments securely
  • Track your progress through coding courses
  • Personalize your AI-guided learning experience
  • Recommend courses and content based on your interests

Communication

  • Send important updates about our platform and services
  • Notify you about changes to our terms or policies
  • Inform you about new courses, features, or special offers

Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Develop new features and improve existing ones
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We respect your privacy and only share your information in limited circumstances:

Payment Processors

We use secure third-party payment processors to handle subscription payments. These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your payment information.

Service Providers

We may engage trusted third-party companies to perform services on our behalf, such as:

  • Hosting and infrastructure services
  • Data analytics and performance monitoring
  • Customer support platforms

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of SkillMynte, our users, or others.

No Data Selling: We do not and will not sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is used solely to provide and improve our services.

5. Cookies and Tracking

SkillMynte uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our platform, and personalize your learning experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember your course progress and preferences
  • Analyze platform usage to improve our services
  • Provide relevant content and recommendations

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect your experience on our platform.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Correction

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Update or correct inaccurate information in your account

Data Management

  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations
  • Download your learning data and progress information
  • Object to certain processing of your information

Communication Preferences

  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Adjust your notification settings in your account

Exercising Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at Skillmynteofficial@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect it:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure servers with regular security updates
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing

Administrative Measures

  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy
  • Incident response plan for potential data breaches

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we continuously work to protect your information.

8. Legal Compliance

SkillMynte operates in compliance with applicable privacy laws and regulations:

Nigerian Data Protection

We adhere to the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and other relevant Nigerian laws governing data privacy and protection.

International Standards

While primarily serving Nigerian users, we follow international best practices for data protection.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes through our platform or via email.
