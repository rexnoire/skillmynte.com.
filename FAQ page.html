General Questions

What is SkillMynte?

SkillMynte is an AI-powered learning platform designed to help you master coding and technology skills. We offer comprehensive courses in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other programming languages, supported by our intelligent AI tutor that provides personalized guidance and feedback.

Our platform is specifically designed for tech learners, with content and pricing tailored to our local market.

How does the AI-guided learning support work?

Our AI learning assistant analyzes your progress, identifies areas where you need improvement, and provides personalized recommendations. It can:

  • Answer your coding questions in real-time
  • Suggest relevant practice exercises based on your skill level
  • Provide instant feedback on your code
  • Adapt the learning path to match your pace and goals

Think of it as having a personal coding tutor available 24/7!

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. If you cancel:

  • Monthly and yearly plans: You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period

We don't lock you into long-term contracts. You're free to cancel whenever you want.

Exception: If you experience technical issues that prevent you from accessing our platform, please contact our support team within 7 days of purchase, and we'll work to resolve the issue or provide a refund if we cannot fix it.

Learning Experience

Do you provide certificates after completing a course?

Yes! When you successfully complete any course on SkillMynte, you'll receive a digital certificate of completion that you can download, print, or share on professional networks like LinkedIn.

Our certificates:

  • Include your name, course title, and completion date
  • Feature a unique verification code for authenticity
  • Are recognized by Nigerian tech employers

These certificates demonstrate your commitment to learning and can enhance your job applications.

Can I access courses offline?

Yes, you can download course materials, videos, and exercises for offline access through our mobile app. However, some features require an internet connection:

  • AI-guided learning support
  • Code submission and feedback
  • Progress synchronization
  • Community forums

We designed this feature specifically for Nigerian learners who may have intermittent internet access.

What coding languages do you teach?

  • Mobile Development: Swift (iOS), Kotlin (Android), React Native

We continuously add new courses based on industry trends and learner demand.
